About Us

Department of Computer Science and Engineering has been consistent in teaching and research activities with an intake of 60 since inception in 2001. The Department offers both UnderGraduate and Post Graduate programs. The Department has been maintaining an activeinteraction with the industries particularly with the Computer Society of India.

We have been functioning since the inception of the Institution transforming students into engineers with technical skills and knowledge. Providing excellent theoretical and practical work makes the students competent to work in the real world, also allowing them to occupy higher positions in core and IT industries. The department has well equipped laboratories and modern facilities. The departments have well experienced and dedicated faculty to impart knowledge to the students.
